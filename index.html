Indian Cricket Academy

* A cricket academy is a training center for aspiring cricket players, where they can receive professional coaching and guidance to improve their skills and prepare for competitive cricket. The academy provides a structured learning environment where players can work on their batting, bowling, fielding, and overall fitness, under the supervision of experienced coaches.Every candidate has to participate in state-level camps, consisting of playing matches across the country on different pitches.
